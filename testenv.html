Born out of Helsinki, Finland and to be relived in Tokyo, Japan, Junction Asia brings hackers together for a 48hr hackathon that is unlike any other hackathon in Japan.

Junction Asia is free for everyone, and we believe that the best solutions come from the most freedom and make creativity the limit of the outcomes. Junction Asia will be officially in English, but also invites Japanese speakers as well to the event.

In Junction Asia, there will be several tracks. In each track, there is a different theme with a challenge. Join one of the tracks of your liking and hack the problem for your chance to win a round-trip to Finland for Slush 2016, a chance to be at Slush Asia 2016 and more!

NEC/Lenovo/Intel track

For whom?

If welcomes all developers, physicians and digital health enthusiasts to co-create both:
- pre-emptive healthcare services to support healthier life and/or
- advanced diagnostics services supporting a smooth recovery when something happens
If is the leading Nordic insurance company and committed to building a safer and healthier society. In order reach the goal, If actively seeks partnerships and collaboration with the start-up community.

Track content

During the event, participants will be creating new innovative health care products and services using relevant data, the leading API’s and devices, e.g. Apple Watch. The track is powered by IBM Bluemix. Bluemix is the leading platform-as-a-service solution and brings connectivity to IBM’s Watson, which in turn has, among others, multiple health related applications.

How to win?

Be creative with the tools given, help society and win!

The Future of Communication w/ SoftBank Group

For whom?

Pepper is a humanoid robot capable of recognising the principal human emotions and adapting his behaviour to mood, personality and preference of the people he is interacting with.
Designed to be a genuine day-to-day companion, Pepper communicates with you in the most natural and intuitive way. In addition to being used in SoftBank Mobile stores, Pepper is also increasingly being adopted in homes.

Reference:
https://www.aldebaran.com/en/cool-robots/pepper
http://pepper-atelier-akihabara.jp/archives/1239

Track content

At the track, SoftBank invites Junction Asia participants to advance the future of communication by using Pepper, be it in a family setting, communication with customers or even connecting people in general.

By joining the Aldebaran developer program and using the Choregraphe application, you will be able to program Pepper creatively within the context of communication.

How to win?

The most innovative idea and the work made for the communication robot wins!
