About us

OP Lounge

Need a break from coding? Want to brainstorm with your team? The OP Lounge will be the perfect place to relax and kick back during Junction. It will be open throughout the weekend for anyone in the need of comfort.

The OP Lounge features Fatboys, couches, coffee, and snacks. It is located in the second floor of the venue with a great view of the whole Hacking Area. Turn your creative chill mode on and come enjoy the perks at the OP Lounge, the OP and Junction crews warmly welcome you!
